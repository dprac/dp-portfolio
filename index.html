About Me

My name is Daven Prachan, and I am a multimedia artist.

My works involve evoking discussions around mental health, personal awareness, and temporality.

The works collected are the result of trial & error, a little luck, and a lot of frustration(pic related).

I am a noob, and coding is not my first, second, or even third language. Consider this a warning.

Project 1

Project 1 is a piece of poetry based on the concept of "uncreative writing".

The poem is based on texts I sent my partner on her way home, and the poem itself is based on our own home, Albany Park.

Project 2

Project 2 involved demonstrating a creative filter or effect for a video call software such as Zoom.

This Project is a demo of how such an effect could be achieved; for this project, I showed myself "no clipping" mid Zoom lecture.

Project 3

Project 3 is a non-linear narrative game built on Twine.

This game, called "Pet the Dog" is an average, run of the mill, dog petting simulatator."

You can play it here: Pet the Dog .

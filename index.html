About Me

My name is Daven Prachan, and I am a multimedia artist.

My works involve evoking discussions around mental health, personal awareness, and temporality.

The works collected are the result of trial & error, a little luck, and a lot of frustration(Pic Related).

I am a noob, and coding is not my first, second, or even third language. Consider this a warning.
